About MediLens

MediLens is a digital solution designed to help users find affordable generic medicines and nearby Jan Aushadhi Kendras.

With features like voice-based search, image scanning via OCR, and instant PDF downloads, MediLens bridges the gap between accessibility and affordability in healthcare.

Whether you're searching for a branded medicine substitute or locating the nearest Kendra, MediLens brings it all to your fingertips.
